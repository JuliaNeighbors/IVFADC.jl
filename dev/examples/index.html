    Usage examples

    Usage examples

    Building an index can be performed with build_index

    julia> using IVFADC

julia> data = rand(10, 10_000);

julia> ivfadc = build_index(data, kc=500, k=256, m=5)
IVFADC Index, 500 coarse vectors, total of 10000-element Float64 vectors, UInt8 codes

    Searching into the index is done with knn_search

    julia> point = data[:, 1234];

julia> knn_search(ivfadc, point, 5)
([1234, 614, 7992, 7449, 9091], [0.163841, 0.316145, 0.334381, 0.340007, 0.36709])

    Results may vary depending on how many clusters are being used to search into

    julia> knn_search(ivfadc, point, 5, w=10)
([1234, 614, 7992, 7449, 9091], [0.163841, 0.316145, 0.334381, 0.340007, 0.36709])

    Adding and removing points is done with

    julia> for i in 1:5
           add_to_index!(ivfadc, rand(10))
       end

julia> ivfadc
IVFADC Index, 500 coarse vectors, total of 10005-element Float64 vectors, UInt8 codes

    and

    julia> delete_from_index!(ivfadc, [1,2,3]);

julia> ivfadc
IVFADC Index, 500 coarse vectors, total of 10002-element Float64 vectors, UInt8 codes

    respectively.
